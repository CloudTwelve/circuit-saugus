About

PCSS II Robotics is a 501(c)(3) non-profit that aims to provide students with the opportunity to develop their technological skills and compete globally, without being limited by their resources or circumstances.

Circuit is the latest hackathon being held by PCSSII Robotics. With a racing theme, attendees build projects over a 2-day period from 8AM to 8PM, competing for big-ticket prizes and learning skills along the way.
